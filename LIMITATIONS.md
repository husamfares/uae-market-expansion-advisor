# Limitations and Future Improvements

Although the system performs well for strategic advisory questions, several improvements could strengthen it further.

The knowledge layer is currently limited and could be expanded with richer market datasets, including more detailed district-level restaurant data and competitor information.

Some recommendations rely on strategic reasoning rather than highly granular market evidence. Future versions could improve this by integrating additional datasets.

A possible next step would be introducing a RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) system to dynamically retrieve information from larger knowledge sources.

Additional improvements could include stronger validation rules, richer competitor intelligence data, and a more advanced interface beyond the CLI.
